# Pokémon Damage Calculator　Compatible game version: Pokémon Champions M-A
## Basic Interface
![Basic Interface](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b55c9a71-30a4-487e-a1ec-2b6f797314d1)

* The top App Bar buttons are (in order): Dark/Light theme toggle / Language switcher (remembers your device setting)
* In the main panel, you can configure Pokémon base stats. This tool follows the new EV system with a maximum of 32 per stat and a default total cap of 66
* After selecting a Pokémon, it will be searched automatically; if the Pokémon is not found, the image will remain as an egg
* ***All languages support English search! Search for Pokémon/moves/items/abilities without switching your input method!***
* Some popular Pokémon will auto-fill their commonly used moves

## Field Effects
![Field Effects](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/37d568f6-8383-4131-a1b3-4cf0eef07bed)
* Weather, terrain, and other settings are located in the right-side panel. Click the corresponding icon to activate them. On desktop they are always visible; on mobile they collapse into a menu triggered by the icon in the top-right corner

## Damage Calculation Interface
![Damage Calculation Interface](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/615f298a-30c0-4543-ba2d-9e106b72bedb)
* By default, Pokémon 1 attacks Pokémon 2. Damage is calculated automatically once both Pokémon and moves are set. The panel can be collapsed to the bottom
* To swap the two Pokémon, click the swap button to the right of the Pokémon selection area

## Save & Load
![Save](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/07db234e-ba15-49ab-892d-195efbf79648)
* In the Pokémon info section, click the icon on the right to save or load previously configured values for repeated use
* You can add a note when saving to record which opponent this Pokémon build is tailored against!

![Load](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/00ee4017-370e-4e11-ad64-1db673aeb1ba)
* Hover over a Pokémon's image while loading to display its note
* When multiple Pokémon are saved, you can drag and drop to reorder them
* ***Load supports importing Pokepaste format!! Up to 6 Pokémon can be imported at once!!***
